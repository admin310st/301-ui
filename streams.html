1 Configure Smart Shield

Block bots and moderators before they reach routing rules.

Smart Shield protects your campaigns by filtering unwanted traffic. Match by geo, device, ASN, TLS fingerprint — and route bots and moderators to safe pages before they reach your TDS rules.

Shield decisions may prevent requests from reaching TDS rules. Configure Shield first to ensure proper traffic flow.

2 Create your first rule

Choose what to match and what to do when the rule matches.

Build routing rules that run top-to-bottom. The first matching rule wins. You can route by geo, device type, traffic source, UTM parameters, and more.

SmartLink

A/B Testing (Multi-Armed Bandit)

Verify rules work correctly before going live.

All edits are saved as drafts. Use the Simulator to test your rules with different geo/device/UTM combinations, then publish to sync the active ruleset to Workers.
